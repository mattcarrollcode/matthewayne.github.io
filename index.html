Matthew

Matthew is a bay area native, born and raised. He grew up playing jazz saxophone, tennis, mountain biking, and tinkering with computers alongside his father and grandfather. He eventually moved south to study Chemical Engineering at UCSB. While once visiting home, Matthew joined a friend for Bible study one Tuesday night and met Rachel! After dating long-distance for 6 months, his love for her and for tech innovation led him to return to Silicon Valley. He now works at Google as a Developer Relations Engineer for YouTube. In the evenings and on the weekends, you can find him reading constantly, learning bass guitar, riding his bike / driving in the mountains, and adventuring with his fiancee, friends, and family.

Rachel

Rachel grew up on the beaches and hills of southern Los Angeles. She developed a love for learning and creating from a very young age, causing her extracurricular activities to evolve constantly. Her love of holistic learning led her north to attend SCU, where she pursued English, history, philosophy, and art. After graduating, she decided to stay in the bay area. While working in retail management at Hugo Boss, she found and joined a new Bible study, the same one Matthew showed up to one Tuesday night, four months later. After three weeks, he asked her on a date, and the story never ended! She now manages the APM Program at Yahoo!, and on the weekends you can find her sewing, taking photos, binge watching TED Talks, running, and engaged in intensely-deep conversations about life with her fiance, closest friends, family and total strangers.
